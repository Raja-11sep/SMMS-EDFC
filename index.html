Privacy Policy

CRIS is committed to protecting your privacy while delivering the software that brings you the convenience you desire in the DFCCIL-SMMS application. This privacy statement governs your use of the DFCCIL-SMMS application for the Android platform. It does not apply to other online or offline CRIS sites, services, or apps.

Collection and Use of Your Information

The DFCCIL-SMMS application does not collect or transmit to CRIS personally identifiable information or require the use of logins, passwords, etc. However, you are offered the opportunity to participate in the App Rating Program. The App Rating collects information about your experience with the application through rating the application. We use this information to improve the quality, reliability, and performance of the mobile application and services.

Security of Your Information

CRIS respects and protects the privacy of the individuals who use CRIS's services and is committed to the security of your information. However, the application is not capturing any individually identifiable information about you.

Changes to this Privacy Statement

We will occasionally update this privacy statement to reflect changes in our products, services, and customer feedback. When we post changes, we will revise the "Last updated" date at the top of this statement. If there are material changes to this statement or if CRIS will use your personal information, we will notify you prior to implementing the change. We encourage you to periodically review this statement to stay informed about how CRIS is protecting your information.
